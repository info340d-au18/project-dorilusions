Potential Applications

Some of our web's potntial applications are:

  • 1. Performances Related Acvitities

      This includes functions such as booking performances, looking up upcoming performances, and getting in touch with the club UW Taiko Kai performs for the students. Thus we want to make sure that they have easy access towards those information. This application of our website allows user to find the information about our performances. This also allows organizations to book appointment with us more easily when they have the need.

  • 2. Account Management

      We want to have a personal portal for our users. The portal helps the user to organize their information. For the general public, they can view the performances they scheduled, they booked, or they plan to attend. For members, they can access club related files such as Meeting Notes, attendance, documents on who's performances, Taiko Kai Constitution. Club directors have more functions as an Admin account. They can manage members, control files, and make changes to certain things on the website. All users have access towards performance videos and some of the basic Taiko techniques.

  • 3. Taiko Related Info

      UW Taiko Kai also aims to educate the general public about the culture of Taiko. Thus, we dedicate this section to the history of Taiko and UW Taiko Kai. The website will contain the history Taiko as a musical instrument in Japan and how it spreads around the world. It will also talk about the history of UW Taiko Kai, as a RSO, its developments, and its current members.
